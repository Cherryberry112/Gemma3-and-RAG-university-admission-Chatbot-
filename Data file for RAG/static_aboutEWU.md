# vision of EWU 

- To augment national capacity for tertiary education and contribute to human capital development.

- To equip graduates with professional skills, innovative and critical thinking to take advantage of emerging opportunities.

- To provide access to affordable university-level education to less-well-to-do sections of society to raise employable skills and improve access to job opportunities.

- To be a top-notch learning center that combines excellence in education with strong human values and social responsibility.

- To promote a knowledge-based society and informed citizenry with respect for pluralism and democracy.

# mission of EWU 

- To become a center of excellence for higher education, making quality postsecondary education accessible at an affordable cost.

- To advance knowledge creation and promote economic, social and technological progress.

- To excel in critical disciplines that address socio-economic challenges and advance creative pursuits.

- To provide a stimulating learning environment where faculty and students can discover, critically examine, preserve and transmit knowledge, values, and wisdom.

- To ensure that students receive the highest quality of education, preparing them for a purposeful life, personally, professionally and socially.
