# East West University – Campus Facilities

# Available Facilities

## Parking
### Description
Parking facility available for students, faculty, and visitors.

## Cafeteria
### Description
Modern cafeteria inaugurated in August 2023 with:
- Capacity to serve 500 consumers simultaneously  
- Ability to prepare food for 10,000 people daily  
- Artistic decoration and central air conditioning  
- Automatic fire extinguishing system  
- Fresh food in a pleasant environment to support student nutrition and focus  

### URL
https://www.ewubd.edu/news/ewu-inaugurated-its-newly-constructed-modern-cafeteria

## Library
### Description
Dr. S. R. Lasker Library serves as the central library, offering:
- Extensive physical book collection  
- Online archives  
- Observance of academic events such as National Library Day and Open Access Week  

### URL
http://lib.ewubd.edu/

## Common Room
### Description
Separate common rooms for boys and girls. Renovated and reopened in April 2024. Features include:
- Table tennis  
- Chess  
- Carom  
- Recreational activities  
- Comfortable seating for relaxation and socialization  

### URL
https://www.ewubd.edu/news/east-west-university-re-opened-its-common-rooms-after-renovations

## Career Counseling Center
### Description
Provides:
- Career guidance

- Workshops and career events

# Facilities Not Available

## Transportation System
### Description
The university does not provide an internal transportation system.  
Students are encouraged to use:
- Public transport  
- Cycling  
- Walking  
to minimize carbon emissions.
