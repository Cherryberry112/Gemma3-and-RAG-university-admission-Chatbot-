# East West University – Payment Procedure

## Payment Modes
- Bank Counter / Booth
- Online Payment Gateway

# Bank Counter / Booth Payment

## Accepted Payment Methods
- Cash
- Pay Order

## Supported Banks
- Bank Asia PLC
- United Commercial Bank PLC
- Brac Bank PLC
- Mutual Trust Bank PLC
- Dutch Bangla Bank PLC

## Notes
- Use Advising Slip (from student portal) or Manual Deposit Slip.
- If you have advance, arrear, scholarship, or waiver, use a Manual Deposit Slip to recalculate the exact payable amount.
- Students must print their own Advising/Deposit Slip from the portal.
- Do not ask bank staff for Advising Slips.
- Avoid large/corporate branches that do not accept utility-bill deposits.
- Agent banking is discouraged (except DBBL).

# Adjustment Rules

## Adjustment Order
1. Scholarship / Financial Aid / Regular Waiver  
2. Special Waiver (20%)  
3. Add other fees (lab fee, library fee, etc.)

## Sample Calculation
- Tuition Fee: 44,100  
- Scholarship/Waiver: 15,000  
- After Adjustment: 29,100  
- Special Waiver (20%): 5,820  
- Net Tuition: 23,280  
- Lab Fee: 1,000  
- Library Fee: 500  
- **Final Amount: 24,780**

# Important Guidelines
- Avoid large/corporate bank branches for deposit.
- Avoid agent banking services (except Dutch Bangla Bank).
- Any advance payment will be adjusted to the next semester.
- No cash refund is permitted.
- Print Advising Slip from student portal if missing.
