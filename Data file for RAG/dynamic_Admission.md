# East West University Admission Guide

## Admission Overview

This document outlines the admission process, application requirements, and fee information for Undergraduate, Graduate, and Diploma Programs at East West University (EWU).

---

## Admission Process

### Undergraduate Programs

- Dates and Deadlines: https://www.ewubd.edu/undergraduate-dates-deadline  
- Application Link: https://admission.ewubd.edu/  
- Instructions: Before starting, read the Instructions and Admission Eligibility carefully.

#### Process Flow
1. Visit www.ewubd.edu or http://admission.ewubd.edu  
2. Click "New Application"  
3. Select program and enter Name / Mobile No / Email ID / HSC passing year  
4. Receive 8-digit EWU Login ID  
5. Sign in with Login ID / Mobile No  
6. Download Pay Slip for cash payment or pay online  

#### Required Documents
- Passport photo: 240x300 pixels  
- Scanned signature: 300x80 pixels

## Application Requirements

### Undergraduate Programs

#### General Requirements
- Minimum GPA 3.00 in SSC & HSC (2.40 for Diploma Engineering)  
- O/A Level Requirements: Minimum 4 Bs and 3 Cs across 7 subjects  
- American High School Diploma accepted (GED not accepted)  

#### Programs Without Specific Subject Requirements
BBA, Economics, English, LL.B, Sociology, Information Studies, Population & Public Health Sciences

#### Subject-specific Requirements
| Program | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| CSE, ICE, EEE | Math & Physics (pass) |
| CE | Math, Chemistry & Physics |
| GEB | Biology (SSC) & Science subjects |
| Math / Data Science | Math required |

#### Bachelor of Pharmacy
- Min GPA total 8.00 (≥ 3.50 each exam)  
- Specific GPA in Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Math  
- A Level: B in Chemistry & Biology, C in Physics & Math  
- PCB registration required within 3 months  
- Foreign applicants must provide equivalence certificate  

---

### Graduate Programs

#### MBA
- Bachelor’s degree, minimum GPA 2.50 (SSC & HSC)  
- EWU CGPA ≥ 3.00 exempt from written test  
- Other university CGPA ≥ 3.50 exempt from test  
- TOEFL ≥ 550 or GMAT ≥ 300 waives test  
- Course Catalog: https://fbe.ewubd.edu/masters-of-business-administration/mba-courses  

#### EMBA
- Bachelor’s degree and minimum 2 years executive experience  
- Admission based on interview  
- Course Catalog: https://fbe.ewubd.edu/masters-of-business-administration/emba-courses  

#### MDS
- Bachelor or Master in any discipline  
- Admission test required; experience preferred  

#### MSS in Economics
- Must have Economics background or take prerequisite courses  

#### MS in Data Science & Analytics
- Four-year degree in Mathematics/Statistics/Engineering/Science/Economics/Business  

#### MS in CSE
- Four-year degree in CSE or related field  
- HSC Science background required  
- Admission test required  

#### MA in English / TESOL
- BA (Hons.) in English or equivalent  
- TESOL accepts candidates from other fields with teaching experience  

#### MPRHGD
- Bachelor's degree in Social/Science field  
- TOEFL ≥ 550 or GRE ≥ 300 waives test  

#### M. Pharm
- Bachelor of Pharmacy with minimum CGPA 2.50  
- Minimum SSC/HSC GPA 2.50  

#### LL.M.
- LL.B. degree with CGPA ≥ 2.50  
- Must pass written and viva test  

---

### Diploma Programs Eligibility

#### Post Graduate Diploma in Entrepreneurship Development
- Minimum CGPA 2.00 in undergraduate degree  
- Must have business idea  

#### Post Graduate Diploma in Population, Public Health & Disaster Management
- Bachelor’s degree in any field  
- TOEFL 550 or GRE 300 waives test
