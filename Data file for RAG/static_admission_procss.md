# East West University Admission Information 


## 1. Undergraduate Admission

### Deadlines
* **Application Deadline:** [www.ewubd.edu/undergraduate-dates-deadline](https://www.ewubd.edu/undergraduate-dates-deadline)

### Eligibility Criteria
**General Requirements:**
* Minimum GPA of 3.00 in both SSC and HSC Examinations.
* Minimum GPA 2.40 in Diploma in Engineering under Bangladesh Technical Education Board.
* **O/A Level:** At least 4 B's in the best 4 subjects and 3 C's in the remaining 3 subjects.

**Program Specific Requirements:**
* **Engineering (CSE, ICE, EEE):** Must have pass grades in Mathematics & Physics in HSC/A Level.
* **Civil Engineering:** Must have Mathematics, Chemistry & Physics in HSC/A Level.
* **Pharmacy:**
    * Minimum aggregate GPA of 8.00 in SSC+HSC (total scale of 10).
    * Minimum GPA of 3.50 in both Chemistry & Biology.
    * Minimum GPA of 3.00 in Physics & Mathematics.
* **Programs with no subject requirement:** BBA, Economics, English, LL.B, Sociology, Information Studies, Population & Public Health Sciences.

### Application Process
**Link:** [https://admission.ewubd.edu/](https://admission.ewubd.edu/)
**Fee:** 1,500 BDT

1.  Visit www.ewubd.edu or http://admission.ewubd.edu.
2.  Click 'New Application'.
3.  Select Program & Enter Name/Mobile No/Email ID/HSC passing year.
4.  Receive an 8-digit EWU Login ID.
5.  Enter EWU Login ID | Mobile No and SIGN IN.
6.  Download Pay Slip for CASH Payment OR Pay Online.

**Payment Methods:** Online (BKash, Visa, Mastercard, Nagad, Rocket) or Cash (EWU Admission Office).

### Required Documents
* Passport size colored photograph (240 W × 300 H pixels).
* Scanned signature (300 W × 80 H pixels).
* SSC and HSC certificate and marksheet.
* Original transcripts.



## 2. Graduate Admission

### Deadlines
* **Application Deadline (MBA & MS in CSE):** 2025-03-20
* **Dates URL:** [www.ewubd.edu/graduate-dates-deadline](https://www.ewubd.edu/graduate-dates-deadline)

### Program Requirements

#### MBA (Master of Business Administration)
* **Eligibility:** Bachelor degree from a reputed university; Minimum GPA of 2.50 in SSC/HSC; Minimum 7 points based on academic history.
* **Test Exemptions:**
    * TOEFL 550+ and GMAT 300+.
    * EWU graduates with CGPA 3.00+.
    * Other university graduates with CGPA 3.50+.

#### Executive MBA
* **Eligibility:** Bachelor degree; Minimum 2 years work experience in an executive position; Minimum GPA of 2.50 in SSC/HSC.
* **Selection:** Based on interview.

#### MS in CSE
* **Eligibility:** 4-year Bachelor in CSE/CS/CE/SE/ICE; Minimum CGPA 2.5 on 4.0 scale; Must pass from Science group in HSC.

#### MA in English
* **Eligibility:** BA (Hons.) in English with minimum 2nd class or CGPA 2.50.

### Admission Tests
* **MBA Admission Test:**
    * **Date:** 2025-03-25 at 2:00 PM.
    * **Subjects:** English Language, Mathematics, General Knowledge.
* **MS in CSE Admission Test:**
    * **Date:** 2025-03-28 at 10:00 AM.
    * **Subjects:** Computer Science fundamentals, Mathematics.

### Application Process
**Fee:** 1,500 BDT (Foreign students: 16 USD).

1.  Visit www.ewubd.edu or https://admission.ewubd.edu/.
2.  Fill online admission form and pay fee.
3.  Submit required documents (Bachelor's degree, Transcripts, Photo, Signature).
4.  Take admission test (if applicable) and attend interview.
