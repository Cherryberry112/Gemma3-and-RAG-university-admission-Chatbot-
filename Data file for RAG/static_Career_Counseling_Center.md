# Career Counseling Center (CCC)

## Basic Information
### Center Name
Career Counseling Center (CCC)

### Established Year
2001

## Mission and Vision
### Mission
To help establish links between prospective employers and East West University students and graduates, serving as a prime service provider in human resource and career development.

### Objectives
- Prepare students for internship placement.  
- Assist with career search and job placement processes.  
- Provide information and guidance for higher study opportunities.  
- Foster career development action plans and enhance personal qualities.  
- Support students in reaching their full potential and promoting social and intellectual growth.

# Core Services and Programs

## Career Development and Advisory Services
- Career counseling and advisory support for students and graduates.  
- Workshops on professional skills (CV & Cover Letter writing, Corporate Grooming).  
- Training sessions and seminars (Career Talks, Etiquette, Personal Behavior and Professionalism).  
- Providing career tips, development resources, and guidance.  
- Linking academic majors to relevant career paths.

## Employment and Networking Services
- Organizing On-Campus Recruitment programs.  
- Hosting Job Fairs (Fall Career Fair, Winter Career Fair).  
- Facilitating internship placement opportunities.  
- Maintaining Job Seeker Registration and EWU Graduates' Profile databases.  
- Coordinating with the Department of Students' Welfare and student welfare clubs.

# Mental Well-Being Services

## Socio-Psyche Counseling Center
### Description
Provides professional psychological counseling to support students in:  
- Managing psychological stress  
- Overcoming personal and family relationship challenges  
- Addressing social and emotional difficulties
