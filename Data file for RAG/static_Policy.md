# East West University – Policy Summaries

# Sustainability Climate Action Policy
- Purpose: Integrate sustainability into all university operations.
- Scope: Applies to all students, staff, faculty, and contractors.
- Key Principles: Energy efficiency, water efficiency, paper reduction, responsible waste management.
- Key Actions: Green construction, reduce food waste, minimize plastics, expand green spaces.

# Sustainable Procurement Policy
- Purpose: Ensure sustainable, high-quality procurement at competitive cost.
- Scope: Applies to all purchases using university funds or grants.
- Key Principles: Align with climate goals, prefer sustainable suppliers, prioritize local sourcing.
- Key Actions: Transparent procurement, best value for money, simplified vendor participation.

# Sustainable Investment Policy
- Purpose: Guide responsible, ethical, ESG-aligned investments.
- Scope: Applies to all university investments and savings.
- Key Principles: ESG alignment, long-term returns, avoid harmful companies, support SDGs.
- Key Actions: Risk assessment, avoid risky banks/NBFIs, no stock-market investment, limit bank exposure.

# Equity, Diversity & Inclusion (EDI) Policy
- Purpose: Promote a diverse, equitable, and inclusive university environment.
- Scope: Applies to all faculty, staff, students, and stakeholders.
- Key Principles: Non-discrimination, inclusive recruitment, accessible learning environment.
- Key Actions: Diversity targets, scholarships for underrepresented groups, curriculum review.

# Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy
- Purpose: Prevent bribery, corruption, and unethical practices.
- Scope: Applies to all EWU community members and stakeholders.
- Key Principles: Zero tolerance, no facilitation payments, strict rules on gifts.
- Key Actions: Report violations, protect whistleblowers, accurate financial records.

# Outreach Projects Policy
- Purpose: Build meaningful community partnerships.
- Scope: Students volunteering in organizations, communities, and NGOs.
- Key Principles: Community improvement, socio-cultural understanding, skill development.
- Key Actions: Mentoring, STEM outreach, arts/culture work, environmental projects, disaster relief.

# Social & Environmental Training Policy
- Purpose: Promote environmental and social responsibility through structured programs.
- Scope: Applies to students, faculty, and staff.
- Key Principles: Culture of responsibility, practical skill development, academic integration.
- Key Actions: Mandatory sustainability courses, student clubs, events, research grants.

# Disciplinary Policy
- Purpose: Maintain standards of conduct for students and employees.
- Scope: Applies to all EWU members.
- Key Principles: Academic integrity, professionalism, confidentiality.
- Key Actions: No political involvement, no unauthorized employment, no misconduct or harassment.

# Plagiarism Policy
- Purpose: Ensure academic honesty and originality.
- Scope: Applies to all academic submissions by students and faculty.
- Key Principles: Clear definition of plagiarism, strict accountability.
- Key Actions: Use plagiarism checking tools, report cases, apply penalties, allow appeals.

# Artificial Intelligence (AI) Policy
- Purpose: Guide ethical and permitted use of AI tools.
- Scope: Applies to all members of the university.
- Key Principles: Ethical use, enforce citation, limit generated content, prohibit deception.
- Key Actions: Allowed for brainstorming/grammar; banned for writing drafts, impersonation, misinformation.

# Carbon Emission Policy
- Purpose: Reduce EWU’s carbon footprint.
- Scope: Covers all campus operations and community members.
- Key Principles: Emission reduction, renewable energy, sustainability in academics.
- Key Actions: Energy audits, green transportation, recycling systems, emission monitoring.

# Academic Grading & Evaluation Policy
- Purpose: Define grading scale and academic rules.
- Scope: Applies to all undergraduate and graduate students.
- Key Principles: Standard grading, academic honesty, fair evaluation.
- Key Actions: CGPA rules, probation/dismissal, retake policy, incomplete & withdrawal rules.
