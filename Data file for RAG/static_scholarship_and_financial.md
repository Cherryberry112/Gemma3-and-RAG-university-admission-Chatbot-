# East West University (EWU) — Scholarships & Financial Assistance
---

## Quick summary
- Annual scholarship fund: **11 Crore BDT**  
- Fund share of earnings: **~9%** annually  
- Target coverage: **≥20%** of regular students  
- Legal minimum (Private University Act 2010): **6%** of enrolled students  
- Freedom fighters quota: **3%** reserved  
- Effective from: **Spring Semester 2025**

---

## Merit scholarships — overview 
- Admission test top scorers (requirement: **≥75%** in admission test): allocations — Business/Econ: **5**, Sciences/Engineering: **4**, Liberal Arts/Social Sci: **2**; continuation: **CGPA ≥3.50**.  
- Graduate admission top scorer (≥75%): **1** scholarship (MBA if ≥20 admitted); continuation **CGPA ≥3.50**.  
- Perfect grades (SSC & HSC all A+ including 4th subject): **100% tuition for 4 years**; continuation **GPA ≥3.50**.  
- O/A-level high achievers (O: **7 A's**, A: **3 A's** in one sitting): **100% tuition for 4 years**; continuation **GPA ≥3.50**.  
- District quota (one poor & meritorious per district): requirement **GPA 5.00** in SSC & HSC; **full tuition + lodging for 4 years**; continuation **CGPA ≥2.60**.  
- GPA-5 holders (SSC 2022 & HSC 2024): **50% tuition waiver first year**; continuation **GPA ≥3.50** per semester.  
- EWU graduates entering grad programs (CGPA ≥4.00): **100% tuition for up to 2 years**; continuation **CGPA ≥3.50**.  
- Foreign students: **50% tuition waiver** (general rule).  
- Top performers (top 10% each batch per dept; after 1/4 degree completed with CGPA ≥3.90): **Full tuition for equal number of credits for next 3 semesters**.  
- Graduate programs (2024 onward): CGPA 4.00 → **100%**, 3.90–3.99 → **75%**, 3.80–3.89 → **50%**; continuation **CGPA ≥3.50** and register **≥6 credits/sem**.

## Trustees' scholarship 
- Per-trustee allocation: **500% tuition fee waiver** (i.e., 100% to 5 students or distributed by trustee).  
- Alternative offer: **Max 45 credits tuition waiver per semester**.  
- Carryover: Unutilized credits can be carried forward within the *same academic year*.

---

## Financial assistance (need-cum-merit) — rules & eligibility
- Undergraduate: must have completed **≥12 credits** and **CGPA ≥2.60**.  
- Graduate: must have completed **≥6 credits** and **CGPA ≥2.80**.  
- Basis: **Need-cum-merit** and subject to continuing requirements and demonstrated need.  
- Family concession: if two siblings or spouse study simultaneously → **50% tuition waiver** for second sibling/spouse; both must maintain minimum passing CGPA; may extend to third sibling; **mutually exclusive** with other scholarships.  
- Freedom fighters: **3% admission quota**; wards of wounded/deceased/financially distressed freedom fighters may receive **up to 100% tuition waiver**; continuation **CGPA ≥2.60**; proof required.

## EWU employee benefits
- Child of employee: **50% tuition waiver** for one child during employee’s tenure.  
- Employee student: **50% tuition waiver** for employee pursuing study at EWU.  
- Continuation: subject to maintaining minimum passing CGPA.

## General conditions 
- Students **cannot** receive multiple scholarships/financial assistance simultaneously.  
- Benefits available only during **regular study time** required for the degree.  
- Students **retaking courses** are **not eligible** for scholarships.  
- Benefits discontinued for disciplinary violations of University Disciplinary Code.  
- Minimum registration per semester to remain eligible:  
  - Undergraduate: **≥3 courses (9 credits)**.  
  - Pharmacy & Law: **≥4 courses (12 credits)**.  
  - Graduate: **≥2 courses (6 credits)**.

## Application & selection process of financial Aid
- Application method: prescribed forms (office).  
- Evaluation & selection: **Need-cum-merit** basis; computerized selection system for transparency.  
- Final decision by: **Financial Assistance Committee**.
