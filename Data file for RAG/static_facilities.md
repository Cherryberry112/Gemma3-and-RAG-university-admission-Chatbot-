# East West University – Campus Facilities

## Library
- Dr. S. R. Lasker Library with large physical and digital collections.
- 29,000+ print books, 100,000+ e-books, 45,000+ e-journals.
- Reading spaces and research support.

## Specialized Laboratories
- Computer labs with high-configuration systems (MATLAB, SPSS, AutoCAD, Oracle).
- Engineering labs: Electrical Circuits, Electronics, Digital Systems, Telecommunication, Networking, Power Systems, VLSI.
- Civil Engineering labs: Surveying, Geotechnical, Environmental, Strength of Materials.
- Science labs: Pharmacy (HPLC, GC, FTIR), Genetic Engineering & Biotechnology, Physics.
- Language Laboratory.

## Research Facilities
- Center for Research & Training (EWUCRT): research hub, seminars, training, conferences.
- Research Hub with modern software (Turnitin, Endnote, SPSS, STATA).

## Classrooms and Learning Spaces
- Air-conditioned classrooms.
- Modern multimedia projectors and teaching aids.

## IT & Digital Services
- High-speed Wi-Fi across campus.
- University Data Centre (IT infrastructure, virtualization, IP telephony).
- Web and email services for students, faculty, and staff.

## Student Welfare & Campus Life
- Department of Student Welfare (DSW) manages 18+ student clubs.
- Cultural programs, seminars, workshops, volunteerism, and leadership activities.

## Career Counseling Center (CCC)
- Career guidance and mentoring.
- Internship and job placement.
- Job fairs, skill workshops, employer networking.

## Medical Centre
- Free on-campus medical services.
- First aid, health check-ups, medical counseling.

## Sports & Recreation
- Indoor games: table tennis, chess, carom.
- Recreational spaces for students.

## Cafeteria
- Large modern cafeteria.
- Serves 500 people at once, cooks for up to 10,000 daily.
- Air-conditioned with updated safety systems.

## Common Rooms
- Separate common rooms for male and female students.
- Includes indoor games and relaxation spaces.

## Prayer Rooms
- Dedicated Islamic prayer rooms for students and staff.

## Parking
- Parking facilities for students, faculty, and visitors.

## Green Spaces & Environment
- Maintained gardens and open spaces.
- Sustainability programs: tree plantation, Environmental Week, green initiatives.

## Other Support Services
- Engineering & Maintenance Department
- Office of Internal Auditor
- Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)
- HR & Logistics

## Facilities Not Available
### Transportation System
- No university transport service.
- Students encouraged to use public transport, cycling, walking.
